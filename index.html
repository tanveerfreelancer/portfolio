FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before working with me

What exactly do you help with?+

Two things, together or separately: SEO-optimized content — blog posts and articles researched and written to rank — and WordPress work, from theme customization to plugin setup.

How much experience do you have?+

Just under a year of hands-on, paid work — focused on writing and ranking content, and customizing WordPress sites for real clients. My own sites, namezpro.com and slangvibes.com, are ongoing proof of that.

Do you only work with WordPress?+

Yes, WordPress is where I specialize — from blog setup to WooCommerce stores like noontoys.store. If your site runs on WordPress, or you're planning to build on it, I can help.

What's your typical turnaround time?+

It depends on scope. A single SEO article usually takes 1–3 days, while a full site customization can take a few days to two weeks. I'll always share a clear timeline before we start.

Do you work with clients outside Pakistan?+

Yes — I've worked with clients in Dubai and manage sites aimed at an international audience. Communication happens over WhatsApp or email, whichever works better for you.

How do I get started?+

Message me on WhatsApp or email with a quick outline of what you need. I'll ask a few questions, share a timeline and rate, and we'll take it from there.
